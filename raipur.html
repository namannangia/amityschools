About Us

Welcome to Amity International School, Raipur

At Amity International School Raipur, we nurture young minds with a perfect blend of academic excellence, character building, and global values. Rooted in the rich legacy of the Amity Group, our school is committed to creating future leaders who are confident, compassionate, and capable of shaping a better world. With world-class infrastructure, innovative teaching methods, and a focus on holistic development, Amity Raipur provides a vibrant environment where every child can thrive and reach their fullest potential.

Academics

1. Overview of Curriculum

CBSE curriculum designed to foster academic excellence, creativity, and critical thinking.

2. Subjects Offered

  • Primary (Class I–V): English, Hindi, Mathematics, EVS, Computer Science, Art & Craft, Physical Education
  • Middle (Class VI–VIII): English, Hindi/Sanskrit, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Computer Science, GK
  • Secondary (Class IX–X): English, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Hindi/Sanskrit/Third Language, IT
  • Senior Secondary (XI–XII):
    • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Computer Science, PE
    • Commerce: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Mathematics/IP, English

3. Teaching Methodology

  • Activity-based learning
  • Smart classes and digital tools
  • Project work and group discussions
  • Regular assessments and personalized feedback

4. Special Academic Features

  • Olympiad & NTSE coaching
  • Language Labs for English and Hindi
  • Remedial Classes and Enrichment Sessions
  • Experiential Learning

5. Academic Achievements

  • 100% board result every year
  • Top ranks in city and state-level exams
  • Awards in inter-school academic competitions

Principal's Message

“Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to the people who prepare for it today” — Malcolm X

As India treads on the path of being a future superpower, its youth will be its biggest strength. Amity, under the visionary leadership and guidance of its Founder President Dr Ashok K. Chauhan and Chairperson Dr (Mrs) Amita Chauhan, strives to nurture creative, compassionate and happy children who can build an intellectual and progressive India of the future. With the ethos of modernity blended with tradition, Amity is a holistic learning ecosystem which encourages young minds to think independently, act collaboratively and behave sensitively. Amity International School, Raipur, exemplifies one such ecosystem which is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure. It seamlessly weaves modern and innovative teaching methodologies with a strong value system rooted in “Abhivaadan Shilayasa.” We believe that personal development is the key difference between merely gaining knowledge and being truly educated. Our holistic approach focuses not only on academics but also on life skills that will help learners flourish as responsible global citizens. We value parents and guardians as our partners in progress in cultivating an ambience where children can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our mission is to provide a safe, caring, and joyful environment that nurtures happy children who will be instrumental in creating a happy family, a happy society, and a happy country.

Ms Arpita Majumdar,

Principal
