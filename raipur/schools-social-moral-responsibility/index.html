SCHOOL’S SOCIAL MORAL RESPONSIBILITY

As a part of our social and moral responsibility, Amity International School Raipur regularly organizes and conducts free eye care camps for every age group. With the support and cooperation of qualified doctors, AIS Raipur has been able to reach out to people in various parts of the society and help them in taking care of themselves and their families in the most efficient way. The entire team of AIS puts in their best efforts to make these camps successful by helping the patients voluntarily.
